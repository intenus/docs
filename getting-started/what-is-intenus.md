# Overview

## What is Intenus?

Intenus Protocol is an **intent-based trading platform** that lets you express what you want to achieve in DeFi without worrying about the complex technical details.

### The Problem We Solve

Today's DeFi ecosystem requires you to:

* Navigate multiple protocols and interfaces
* Understand complex trading parameters
* Manually compare prices across different platforms
* Risk front-running and sandwich attacks
* Pay high gas fees for suboptimal routes

### Our Solution

Intenus introduces a revolutionary approach:

1. **Express Your Intent**: Tell us what you want in simple terms ("Swap 1,000 SUI for USDC with minimal slippage")
2. **AI Finds the Best Path**: Our network of competing solvers proposes optimal execution strategies
3. **Verified Execution**: AI agents rank solutions in secure environments and execute the best one
4. **You Get the Best Deal**: Automatically receive superior pricing, lower fees, and maximum security
