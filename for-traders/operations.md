# Operations

Intenus supports a comprehensive range of DeFi operations.

### Spot Trading

Execute immediate token swaps with optimal routing.

**Example Use Cases**:

* Swap SUI for USDC
* Convert between any supported token pairs
* Multi-hop trades (e.g., SUI → USDC → ETH)

***

### Limit Orders

Set price targets and let Intenus execute when conditions are met.

**Example Use Cases**:

* Buy USDC when SUI reaches $2.50
* Sell SUI if price drops below $2.00
* Take-profit and stop-loss orders

***

### Lending & Borrowing

Access the best lending rates across multiple protocols.

**Example Use Cases**:

* Lend SUI at the highest APY available
* Borrow USDC with optimal collateral efficiency
* Automatically rebalance between lending protocols

***

### Cross-Chain Bridging

Move assets between blockchains efficiently.

**Example Use Cases**:

* Bridge USDC from Ethereum to Sui
* Move assets to L2s with best rates
* Cross-chain arbitrage opportunities

***

### Complex Strategies

Combine multiple operations in a single intent.

**Example Use Cases**:

* "Swap SUI for USDC and lend it at the best rate"
* "Borrow USDC, swap for SUI, and stake"
* Custom DeFi strategies in plain language
